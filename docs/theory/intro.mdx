# Introduction

:::warning
Disclaimer: I do not have a formal educational background in music, therefore all the information contained
in this wiki will be of my own understanding and how I apply it.

This is also **NOT** a course, so the contents and their organization might not be
optimal.
:::

Hi, welcome to **Kirisame's Unofficial Music Wiki**, which is a knowledge base for lots of things related
to music theory such as:

- **Scales** (Planned)
- **Chords and their voicings** (WIP)
- **Chord Progressions** (Planned)
- **Modulations** (Planned)
- and many more!

:::
